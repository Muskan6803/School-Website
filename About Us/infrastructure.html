Campus

Labs

At Springdale Public School, we understand the importance of practical learning and hands-on experiences in fostering a deeper understanding of concepts. Our school is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories designed to facilitate experiential learning across various disciplines. Here are some of the specialized laboratories available to our students:

  • Science Laboratories:Our science laboratories are well-equipped with modern instruments and apparatus to conduct experiments across physics, chemistry, and biology. Students have the opportunity to explore scientific principles through hands-on activities, fostering curiosity and critical thinking.
  • Computer Laboratories:Our computer labs are equipped with the latest technology and software to support computer-based learning and programming. Students can develop their digital literacy skills, explore coding languages, and engage in multimedia projects under the guidance of our experienced faculty.
  • Science Laboratories: Fully equipped to facilitate hands-on experimentation and scientific exploration.
  • Language Laboratories:Designed to enhance language proficiency, our language labs are equipped with audio-visual resources and interactive software. Students can practice listening, speaking, and pronunciation skills in a simulated environment conducive to language learning.
  • Mathematics Laboratory:The mathematics lab provides a hands-on approach to learning mathematical concepts through activities and manipulatives. Students engage in collaborative problem-solving and visualization exercises that deepen their understanding of mathematical principles.
  • Robotics and Engineering Labs:Our robotics and engineering labs provide students with opportunities to design, build, and program robots and prototypes. Through project-based learning, students explore STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) concepts and develop practical skills in innovation and engineering.
  • Environmental Science Lab:Dedicated to exploring ecological and environmental concepts, our environmental science lab supports practical learning in sustainability and conservation. Students conduct experiments related to environmental issues, biodiversity, and resource management, contributing to their awareness of global environmental challenges.


Library

Our Library is well endowed with a large collection of books in all the functional areas & well-equipped Senior library and a Junior Library. These includes a range of encyclopedias reference books classics and present day fiction. These are extremely useful in helping students with reference projects, deemed mandatory by the CICSE. A plethora of Indian and international books and periodicals are available to help students keep abreast of current affairs.

Facilities and Resources:
  • Extensive Collection:Our library houses a diverse collection of books, including fiction, non-fiction, reference materials, and periodicals. Whether students are researching for projects or exploring new genres for leisure reading, they will find a wide array of choices.
  • Digital Resources:In addition to traditional books, the library offers access to digital resources such as e-books, online databases, and educational websites. These resources support research, enhance learning, and broaden students' access to information beyond the physical collection.
  • Study Areas:The library provides comfortable and quiet study areas where students can concentrate on their studies individually or collaborate on group projects. These spaces are conducive to both academic focus and creative exploration.
  • Technology:Equipped with computers and internet access, the library supports digital literacy and research skills development. Students can utilize these resources for online research, multimedia projects, and accessing educational software.
  • Librarian Support:Our dedicated librarian is available to assist students in finding resources, conducting research, and developing information literacy skills. They offer guidance on effective use of library resources and promote a love of reading and lifelong learning.


Transport

The School has its own fleet of deluxe air-conditioned buses. All the buses are running on CNG mode and are equipped with the latest communication facilities like mobile phones – in case of any delays or unforeseen events, a message gets flashed to the school, which in turn informs the parents, and initiates action for backup transportation measures, if required. Each bus has a driver, conductor and a security guard, for complete safety and security of the children. The routes and timings of the entire fleet of buses are monitored by a well trained staff.
